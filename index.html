About Us

Casa dos Açores da Bermuda Ltd. (Friends of the House of the Azores of Bermuda) Charity # 967 ("CAB") – was established as a non-profit organization for the purposes of promoting and preserving the Azorean heritage and culture in Bermuda and to preserve the historical significance and contribution that Azoreans and Azorean descendants have made to Bermuda. The entity was registered as a local company with limited guarantee for charitable purposes on 10 March 2015.

More directly, the mandate of CAB, as a non-profit organisation, is to promote and carry out activities which celebrate the Azorean culture with the Bermudian community as a whole so that our community understand who we are, where we came from and the contributions made by Azorean descendants to Bermuda since the mid 1800s. As the center point of our organisation is the Azorean culture and history, our activities will be based on celebrating those two important aspects. Also, as we understand that the Azorean community is a small part of a of Bermuda's people, we have made it part of our objectives to support local charities that benefit all Bermudians regardless of their family origins.

It is important to us to disprove the common perception that the Azorean community is a closed community and are unwelcoming to others. As such CAB opens its doors to all of Bermuda and invites you to discover and celebrate all that the Azorean culture in Bermuda has to offer.

On 11 September 2016, the World Council of the Houses of the Azores has decided to admit ACAB subject to ACAB conforming to its name Casa dos Açores da Bermuda until the next General Meeting.
